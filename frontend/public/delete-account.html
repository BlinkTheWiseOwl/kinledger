Account Deletion Request

At KinLedger, you have full control over your personal and family medical data. We offer two simple methods to permanently delete your account and associated profiles.

Method 1: Delete Instantly In-App (Recommended)

If you still have the KinLedger application installed on your device, you can delete your account instantly:

  1. Open the KinLedger app and log in.
  2. Open the side menu (hamburger icon) and go to your profile/account settings.
  3. Tap the Delete Account option.
  4. Confirm your decision. All your account information and family profiles will be permanently deleted from our servers immediately.

Method 2: Request Deletion via Web/Email

If you have already uninstalled the application or cannot access your account, you can request that we manually delete your account and all associated data.

Please send an email from the email address registered with your KinLedger account to:

Email Address: support.kinledger@gmail.com

Subject Line: DELETE MY ACCOUNT

Request Deletion via Email (Launch App)

Note: Manual deletion requests are processed within 24 to 48 hours. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and all associated medical/profile records have been permanently purged from our databases.
